# CreativeSpaces — Startup HQ

> Two-sided marketplace connecting independent music artists with recording studios and audio professionals. [[Wahhab]] is Co-Founder & Head of Product (40% equity).

---

## What It Is

CreativeSpaces is a mobile-first booking platform for the music production industry. Artists discover and book recording studios and hire audio engineers/producers through a single app. Revenue model: 10% service fee on all bookings.

**Team:** Nicole Sanzio (Founder/CEO, 60%) + [[Wahhab]] (Co-Founder/Head of Product, 40%)
**Status:** Pre-launch — PRD complete, high-fidelity prototype built, revenue model projected
**Tech Stack:** Next.js PWA, Node.js, PostgreSQL + Redis, Stripe Connect
**Priority:** 🟡 Long-term (not current revenue focus — [[NeverMisleads]] hits $3k MRR first)
