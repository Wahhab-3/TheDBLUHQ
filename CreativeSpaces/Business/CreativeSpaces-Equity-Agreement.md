## Key Terms

### Venture Structure
Self-funded grassroots venture with no current venture capital intent. All operations funded by personal contributions and sweat equity. The venture operates as an unincorporated collaboration until formal entity formation.

### Equity Split
- Founder (Nicole Sanzio): **60%** — Retains majority ownership and operational control
- Co-Founder (Wahhab): **40%**

### Vesting Schedule
- **4-year vesting period** with a **1-year cliff**
- Monthly vesting after the cliff (1/36th of remaining shares per month)
- Vesting begins on the Effective Date (February 19, 2026)
- Cliff date: February 19, 2027
- If either party departs before the cliff: forfeits all unvested equity

### Wahhab's Role: Head of Product & Technical Strategy
Responsibilities include: product architecture and design, requirements documentation, technical roadmap planning, developer coordination (when applicable), and monetization strategy advisory.

### Authority Limitations (Co-Founder)
The Co-Founder cannot independently: bind the company to agreements, incur expenses above agreed thresholds, hire contractors or employees, alter the equity structure, or pursue fundraising activities. All major decisions require Founder approval.

### Intellectual Property
All work product created for CreativeSpaces is work-for-hire and assigned to the company. Both parties retain ownership of pre-existing IP brought into the venture.

### Decision-Making
Day-to-day operations: Founder has final authority. Strategic decisions (pivots, fundraising, equity changes): require mutual agreement. Deadlock resolution: 30-day good-faith negotiation, then mediation.

### Exit & Dissolution
Either party may exit with 60 days written notice. Departing party retains vested equity only. Remaining party has right of first refusal on unvested shares. In dissolution: assets distributed per equity percentages after settling obligations.
