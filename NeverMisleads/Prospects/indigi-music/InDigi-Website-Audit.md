# InDigi Music — Website Audit & Analysis

> Prepared by [[NeverMisleads]] for DBLU HQ | March 2026
> Website: www.indigimusic.com

---

## 1. Business Overview

InDigi Music is a music licensing, composition, and clearance company founded by Nicole Sanzio in 2006. It operates as a division of Multi Music & Media Group, LLC, based in New York City (340 W. 42nd St., #2614, New York, NY 10036).

The company positions itself as a premiere destination for TV, film, and media professionals who need music for their projects. They boast a production catalogue of nearly 20,000 pre-cleared indie tracks and work with independent artists, writers, and composers.

**Founder:** Nicole Sanzio, Creative Director
**Parent Company:** Multi Music & Media Group, LLC
**Location:** New York, NY

### Services Offered

- **Composer Services** — Custom music creation from scratch for ads, film, TV, video, interactive, and mobile
- **Music Library / Production Services** — Over 8,000 indie tracks across all genres
- **Music Licensing** — Rights & clearances for footage, photos, print materials, and popular music
- **Music Cue Sheet Assistance** — Reporting to BMI/ASCAP/SESAC for broadcasters
- **Music Supervision** — Finding and clearing music for TV/film/interactive/corporate projects
- **Music Production** — Audio/digital editing, mixing, mastering, and video editing outsourcing

## 2. Brand Colors

The dominant brand identity is built around an orange accent on a dark/black background, creating a music-industry nightlife aesthetic.

| Color | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| InDigi Orange | #E26B37 | Primary accent |
| Near Black | #0F0F0F | Background |
| Dark Charcoal | #303030 | Cards |
| White | #FFFFFF | Body text |
| Medium Gray | #9F9F9F | Subtle text |
| Pink/Magenta | #C94487 | Accent |
| Light Blue | #6CB2EB | Links |
| Darker Blue | #1D68A7 | Hover |

**Aesthetic Direction:** Dark, sleek, and music-industry professional. The diagonal stripe pattern on the dark background gives a textured, premium feel common in entertainment and nightlife branding. The orange accent is bold and energetic. However, the execution falls short due to outdated web design patterns, inconsistent spacing, and a lack of modern UI conventions.

## 4. Critical Issues

1. **Broken Google Maps Embed (Contact Page)** — Large blank/broken white area. Looks unprofessional.
2. **No Contact Form** — Only shows phone, address, email. TV/film professionals need frictionless outreach.
3. **Placeholder Images on Featured Talent** — ALL 6 artist cards use the exact same stock photo labeled "Artist 1" through "Artist 6." Major credibility issue.
4. **Homepage Audio Element Error** — "Your browser does not support the audio element" message in nav area.
5. **Inconsistent Branding on Contact Page** — Bright blue, green, and yellow color blocks completely break from the dark orange identity.
6. **Copyright Year** — Footer shows 2025, needs dynamic updating.

---

## 5. What Could Function Better

- **Services Page Layout** — Walls of text. Needs visual cards with icons, images, and clear CTAs.
- **Music Discovery / Search** — External SourceAudio link should be integrated more seamlessly.
- **Video Embeds** — Small YouTube embeds are not engaging. Custom audio/video player would be better.
- **Mobile Responsiveness** — Fixed-width patterns may not scale well on modern devices.
- **Newsletter Signup** — Minimal "Go" button. Needs better CTA with value proposition.
- **Social Media Integration** — Icons only in footer and small. Twitter/X link uses old branding.
- **About Page Depth** — Nicole's credentials (EMI, BMI, VH1, MTV, Fuse, Showtime, Sony BMG) are buried in paragraph text. Should be visually highlighted.

---

## 6. Recommended Pages for Website Rebuild

### Core Pages (Must-Have)

1. **Home** — Full-width hero with video background, featured tracks player, client logos, strong CTA
2. **About** — Company story, Nicole's bio with credential highlights, team section, timeline
3. **Services** — Visual service cards with icons/images, descriptions, individual CTAs
4. **Music Library / Browse Music** — Integrated search with genre filters, mood tags, inline audio previews
5. **Featured Artists / Roster** — Real artist profiles with photos, bios, and sample tracks
6. **Contact** — Professional contact form, working map embed, brand-consistent styling

### Additional Recommended Pages

7. **Portfolio / Placements** — Showcase TV/film placements for credibility
8. **Blog / News** — SEO, industry news, artist spotlights, new releases
9. **FAQ** — Licensing questions, pricing, process
10. **For Artists / Submit Music** — Clear page for roster applications
11. **Client Login / Dashboard** — Modern login portal

---

## 7. Summary

InDigi Music has a strong business foundation — nearly 20 years in the industry, impressive credentials, a large catalog, and a clear niche. However, the current website does not reflect the caliber of the business. Biggest opportunities: fix broken elements, modernize design while keeping dark + orange identity, add contact form, build credibility pages, integrate music library, create blog for SEO.
