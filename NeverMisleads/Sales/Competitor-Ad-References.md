## How to Use These References

- **Study competitor messaging:** What pain points are they highlighting? How do they frame the solution?
- **Analyze creative formats:** Video vs. carousel vs. image? Testimonial-based or benefit-based?
- **Examine targeting angles:** What audiences are they going after? Broad or narrow targeting?
- **Copy research:** What headlines and calls-to-action are they using? What's the tone?
- **Iterate faster:** Use these as inspiration for your own [[Facebook-Lead-Ad-Campaign]] without reinventing the wheel.
