## Key Principles

- **Lead with the credential:** The 10/10 is your credibility anchor. Use it every time.
- **Personalize:** Reference their specific role and why it matters to your proposal.
- **No hard sell:** This is about building a relationship and getting their perspective.
- **Respect their time:** Offer a specific, short conversation (15 min).
- **Follow the sequence:** First message → wait 3 days → 24-48 hr follow-up → wait 5 days → final touch.
