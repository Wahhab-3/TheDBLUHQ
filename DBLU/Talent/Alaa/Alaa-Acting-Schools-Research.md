# On-Camera Performance & Acting Training for Alaa Farrag — Research Brief

> Comprehensive research on 20+ acting schools, on-camera workshops, and performance coaching in Egypt for [[Alaa-Farrag-Roadmap|Alaa Farrag]]. Prepared March 31, 2026.

---

## Executive Summary

Egypt offers several viable options for on-camera performance training suited to content creators, ranging from intensive studio-based bootcamps to workshops and professional coaching. The strongest options are Arab Film School (structured 5-week program with camera focus), Cairo Acting and Casting Studio (intensive bootcamp with Hollywood-trained coach), Luke Lehner Studios (flexible, method-based in Maadi), and Studio ZAT (practical on-camera with Meisner Technique). For content-specific training, Cairo Photo Club and AUC's Online Content Creation Workshop address platform strategy and video production.

## Cairo-Based Acting Schools (Screen/Camera Focus)

### 1. Arab Film School — Acting with Gerald James
Location: 27 Abd El Wahab El-Qady, Ard el Golf, Cairo. Format: 5-week intensive, 4 days/week, 4-5 hours/day. Curriculum covers Strasberg technique, monologue work, on-camera preparation, understanding camera frame. Contact: +20 10 98601564, info@arabfilmschool.com

### 2. Cairo Acting and Casting Studio (C.A.S) — Actor's Bootcamp
Run by Lauren Lou Geoffroy, Hollywood-trained casting director. 3 levels of bootcamp, improv classes, scene work, private coaching. International casting network (Michael Madsen, Eric Stoltz, Nicolas Cage). Contact: cairoactingstudio@hotmail.com

### 3. Luke Lehner Studios
Location: Maadi, Cairo. Founded 2017. Lehner: 20+ years theatrical experience, MFA from American Repertory Theater at Harvard, trained at Moscow Art Theatre School. Intensive acting workshops, Chubbuck Technique, improv, scriptwriting, private coaching. Contact: lukelehnerstudios.com

### 4. Studio ZAT — Shady Khalaf
1-month intensive. Meisner Technique and camera handling. London Film School trained. Improvisation, body language, using five senses. Contact: studiozat.com

### 5. CrEd (Creative Education International)
Locations: New Cairo, 6th of October, Sheikh Zayed, Maadi, Zamalek, Dokki, Nasr City, Sherouk City. Operating nearly 20 years. Drama classes, musical theatre, Trinity College London exam prep. Note: primarily drama/theatre-focused — verify on-camera offerings. Contact: credinternational.com

### 6. Muse Art House
Location: Dokki, Cairo. Acting workshops, filmmaking, cinematography, screenwriting. Also operates as a talent agency. Founded by Mohamed Sabri and Sameh Sabri. Contact: musearthouse.com

### 7. Pro Step Film School
Location: Agouza, Cairo (est. 2012). Acting workshops, filmmaking, screenwriting, TV/radio presenting, cinematography. One of Egypt's biggest private film schools. Contact: facebook.com/prostepfilmschool

## On-Camera Workshops & Short Courses

### Cairo Photo Club
Egypt's largest hub for photographers, videographers, and content creators (since 2011). 50,000+ creatives community. Mobile Content Creation for Business workshop, general videography/editing, one-to-one coaching. Contact: cairophotoclub.com

### Cairo Ad School
Strategic digital content creation, video production, lighting, filmmaking. Contact: cairoadschool.com

### Photopia Cairo
Hands-on videography bootcamp. Professional studio with cameras, lighting, on-the-spot critique. Contact: photopiacairo.com

## TV Presenter / Host Training

### Cairo Media School
Giza, Cairo. Egypt's first media school specializing in international media development. Two studios with professional equipment. Contact: cairomediaschool.org

### TV Presentation & Public Speaking Specialist Program
Faculty of Mass Communications, Cairo University. 10-day intensive: 4 hours remote lectures + 6 hours in-person practical studio work daily.

### AUC MA in Television and Digital Journalism
Full MA program. Hands-on TV journalism: editorial skills, scriptwriting, interviews, hosting, anchoring, field reporting, voice coaching. Contact: aucegypt.edu
