# Wahhab — Brand Brochure

> Visual brand brochure for [[Wahhab]] / [[DBLU]]. Originally a tri-fold brochure PDF with the brand narrative, duality concept, discography highlights, and DBL/YOU merch line.

---

## The Narrative

> "From Here and There, To Everywhere."

Wahhab is a daring creative, artist, and creative director shaped by movement between cultures, languages, and worlds. Inspired by Arab identity and his experience as an immigrant, his work explores discipline, tension, and growth through sound, visuals, and storytelling.

Rather than chasing trends, Wahhab builds with intention. Each release carries weight, drawing from heritage, personal evolution, and the realities of diaspora life. His work bridges Arabic and English not as a novelty, but as a natural extension of identity.

This is art built for longevity.

---

## The Duality

### Wahhab — The Artist

Wahhab is the poet and creative. Brutally honest, emotionally driven, and fully present in the moment. He creates freely, lives fast, and follows instinct, often without regard for consequence. This is where the raw art comes from.

### DBLU — The Director

DBLU is the business front. The organizer, the hustler, the one who picks up the slack. He pushes productivity, structure, and execution, ensuring the work survives beyond inspiration.

> "Wahhab rejects structure, DBLU resents chaos."

## DBL/YOU Merch

### The Buckets
The bucket hat is a simple, recognizable base for the brand. It allows multiple designs without losing identity.

### Visual Identity
Inspired by football shirts, the front follows a fixed system that makes the brand instantly recognizable. The DBL/YOU logo sits on the left chest, the song name on the right, with a tag placed at the bottom left.

### Artwork
The back artwork is unique to each song and designed as a standalone visual. Every piece is crafted by Wahhab, turning the garment into a physical extension of the music.

> "Crafted by DBLU. Worn by DBL/YOU."

## Visual Description (Original PDF)

The brochure uses the DBLU brand colors: burgundy/dark red background with gold/yellow text and accents. Geometric Islamic-inspired pattern overlay. Features photos of Wahhab, album artwork for all three singles, illustrations of the Wahhab and DBLU characters, bucket hat product photos, and football-shirt-inspired tee mockups (white and olive/khaki). Center panel has a QR code linking to Wahhab's world. The DBLU logo (stylized "W" with crown motif) appears on the cover panel.
