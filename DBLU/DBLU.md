## What It Is

DBLU is the artist brand umbrella for [[Wahhab]] and Alaa. Bilingual hip-hop rooted in Arab identity and cultural resilience. Not a mainstream play — a legacy play.

**Artists:** Wahhab (Abdulwahab) + Alaa (Alaa Farrag)
**Sound:** Arabic/English hip-hop, cultural fusion
**Colors:** Burgundy `#410000` · Gold `#FFB400`
