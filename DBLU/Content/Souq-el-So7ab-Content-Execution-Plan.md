# Souq el So7ab — Content Strategy & Execution Plan

> Week-by-week content execution plan for [[Wahhab]]'s "Souq el So7ab" campaign. Extracted from a 5-sheet XLSX workbook covering overview, weekly execution, content batching, platform strategy, self-evaluation rubric, and infrastructure checklist.

## Content Types

### Visual Fragments (Animated)
Wahhab's signature animated short-form pieces — stylized, culturally rich visual content. Produced in batch sessions during the first week of each month (April, May, June). 2 pieces for older songs in the initial batch, refined based on April data for subsequent months.

### IRL Content
Real-world footage: behind-the-scenes studio sessions, day-in-the-life clips, street culture moments, merch styling. Shot in the second week of each month. Humanizes the brand and builds authenticity.

### Trial Reels
Daily short-form content in Arabic using direct, conversational language. Designed to feel exclusive and personal — intentionally discouraging mass resharing to maintain perception of one-to-one communication.

### Stories Blitz (Release Day)
20-40 Instagram/TikTok Stories on release day: countdowns, behind-the-scenes of the release moment, fan reactions, streaming screenshots, street team updates from Egypt.

## Platform Strategy

| Platform | Posting Cadence | Primary Content | Key Metrics |
|---|---|---|---|
| TikTok | 2+ Reels/day | Trial Reels, VFs, cultural comedy | Views, shares, comments, follower growth |
| Instagram Reels | 2-3 Grid/week + Stories | VFs, IRL, merch styling | Saves, shares, engagement rate, email signups |
| YouTube Shorts | 2-3/week | VFs, IRL clips | Subscriber growth, watch time |
| Instagram Stories | Daily | Behind-the-scenes, polls, countdowns | Replies, sticker taps |

### Four Content Pillars
1. Relatable comedy and social commentary
2. Edutainment and cultural quick tips
3. Cultural pride and modern heritage showcases
4. Day-in-the-life glimpses

All content uses bilingual language mixing (Arabic/English/Franco-Arab) to maximize reach across local Egyptian audiences and the diaspora.

## Weekly Execution Schedule

### Pre-Launch: Week 1 (Mar 26 – Apr 5)
- Animated batch production (2 pieces for older songs)
- Cover art and visual identity finalized
- IRL batch filming
- Website + email signup completed

### Build Phase: Weeks 2-6 (Apr 6 – May 10)
- Content posting begins: 2-3 Grid/week + ~2 Reels/day
- Data analysis after each batch
- Reply to every comment
- Pre-save campaign launches (Apr 13)
- Street team recruitment begins (Apr 13)

### Ramp Phase: Weeks 7-9 (May 11 – Jun 5)
- Press & playlist outreach (May 4 – May 17: SceneNoise, CairoScene, Rolling Stone ME)
- Rally List building + Dave Strategy DMs (May 4 – Jun 5)
- Ecosystem cleanup — all profiles aligned for release (May 25 – Jun 5)
- June batch: 20-30 pieces produced (Jun 1 – Jun 5)
- Content volume ramps to maximum

### Release: Week 10 (Jun 6)
- Release day: 20-40 Stories Blitz
- Rally List activation (personal messages with streaming link)
- Live stream on Instagram/TikTok
- All platforms posting simultaneously

### Post-Release: Weeks 11-14 (Jun 8 – Jul 6)
- Post-release push: fan reactions, UGC reposts, behind-the-scenes of the making
- Live streams + TikTok/IG lives
- Street team competition continues
- Street team winners announced Jul 6
- Data analysis and Q3 planning

## Self-Evaluation Rubric (100 Points)

The workbook includes a 100-point self-evaluation scoring system for tracking execution quality week over week. Categories cover content volume, engagement quality, posting consistency, audience growth, and strategic milestone completion.

## Infrastructure Checklist

23 tasks with deadlines covering pre-campaign setup:

Key items include: Spotify for Artists claimed, all social bios updated, link-in-bio configured (Linktree/custom), website launch with email capture, pre-save page created, merch store configured (POD), Google Drive shared folder set up, analytics tracking configured across all platforms, press kit prepared, street team WhatsApp group created, QR codes generated for physical materials.

## Posting Schedule

Posts go live **Thursday, Friday, Saturday, and Sunday at 12:00pm MST** starting Week 2 (Apr 6 onward).

**Transition Week (Apr 3–5):** Posting begins mid-week before the regular Thu–Sun cadence kicks in.
- Friday Apr 3: Carousel post — 12:00pm MST
- Saturday Apr 4: 1–2 posts — 12:00pm MST
- Sunday Apr 5: 1 post — 12:00pm MST

From Week 2 (Apr 6) onward, all posts follow the Thu/Fri/Sat/Sun at 12:00pm MST schedule.
