# Templates

> Reusable note templates for the vault.

---

## Available Templates

- **Client Profile** — For new NeverMisleads clients
- **Music Project** — For new DBLU music projects
- **Meeting Notes** — Quick capture for calls and meetings
- **Weekly Review** — Weekly check-in template

---

*Create template files in this folder and use Obsidian's Templates plugin to insert them into new notes.*
