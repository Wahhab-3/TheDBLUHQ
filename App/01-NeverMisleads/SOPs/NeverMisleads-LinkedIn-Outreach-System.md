## THE MESSAGE SEQUENCE

### Connection Request Note (300 char limit — make it count)

**Template A — The Compliment + Curiosity**
> Hey [First Name] — came across [Business Name] and love what you're building. I work with local businesses on their digital presence and had a quick idea for you. Would love to connect.

**Template B — The Industry Angle**
> Hey [First Name] — I work with [industry] businesses on branding, websites, and automation. Noticed [something specific about their profile/business]. Would love to connect and share some thoughts.

**Template C — The Mutual Connection**
> Hey [First Name] — saw we're both in the [Orlando/Arizona] business community. I run NeverMisleads, a branding and marketing agency for local businesses. Let's connect.

**IMPORTANT**: Rotate templates. Don't send the same one to everyone. LinkedIn flags repetitive messages.

---

### Message #1 — The Day After They Accept (Warm, Not Salesy)

> Hey [First Name], thanks for connecting! I checked out [Business Name] — [one specific genuine compliment about their work/business].
>
> Quick question — are you happy with how your business looks online right now? Website, Google presence, all that? Just curious because it's what I do for local businesses and I noticed a few things that could help.
>
> No pitch, just a conversation.

**Why this works**: You're asking a question, not selling. Most business owners know their online presence is weak. They'll tell you.

---

### Message #2 — If They Respond Positively (The Hook)

> Appreciate the honesty. Here's what I do that's different from every other agency out there —
>
> Before we even have a first meeting, I study your brand, your Instagram, your current presence, and I build your website. So when we sit down on a Google Meet, you're not looking at a pitch deck — you're looking at your business, already looking professional.
>
> No deposit. No guessing. You see exactly what you're getting.
>
> Want me to pull up [Business Name] and show you what I'd put together? Takes me a night, and it's on me either way.

**Why this works**: This is your killer hook. Nobody else does this. Let it land.

---

### Message #3 — If They Say Yes (Lock It In)

> Perfect. Send me your Instagram handle (or website if you have one) and I'll get started tonight.
>
> Let's set up a quick 15-min Google Meet for [suggest 2 specific times within 48 hours]. I'll screen-share the site and walk you through everything.
>
> What works better for you?

---

### Message #4 — If They Go Silent After Message #1 (Gentle Nudge, Day 3-4)

> Hey [First Name] — no worries if you're busy, I know how it is running a business. Just wanted to circle back in case my message got buried.
>
> I took a quick look at [Business Name] online and had some ideas. Happy to share if you're interested — totally free, no strings.

---

### Message #5 — The Breakup (Day 7-10, Last Attempt)

> Hey [First Name] — I'll keep it short. I put together some ideas for [Business Name] that I think could really help your online presence and save you time. If you ever want to see them, just shoot me a message.
>
> Either way, wishing you the best with the business. It looks like you're doing great work.

**Stop after this. Don't be annoying. Move on to the next prospect.**

## CONTENT STRATEGY (Optional But Powerful)

If you have 10 extra minutes a day, post on LinkedIn. This makes your outreach 3x more effective because people check your profile before responding.

### Post Ideas (Rotate These Weekly)
1. **The Process Post**: "I just built a website for a [type of business] owner. They haven't paid me a dollar yet. Here's why I do that..."
2. **The Before/After**: Show a business's old online presence vs. what you built (get permission)
3. **The Insight Post**: "Most local businesses lose $X/month from missed calls. Here's the math..."
4. **The Story Post**: "I started NeverMisleads because I noticed every local business I walked into had the same problem..."
5. **The Automation Post**: "This [barber/contractor/shop] was spending 3 hours a day on scheduling. Now it's automated. Zero missed appointments."

### Rules
- No hashtag spam (2-3 max)
- Write like you talk
- First line = the hook (people decide to click "see more" based on it)
- Post between 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM for best reach

## RULES TO LIVE BY

1. **Personalize every connection request.** Generic = ignored.
2. **Never pitch in the connection request.** Connect first, talk second.
3. **Check their profile before messaging.** Mention something specific.
4. **Speed kills.** When someone responds, reply within hours, not days.
5. **Don't argue with "no."** Thank them and move on.
6. **Log everything.** If it's not tracked, it didn't happen.
7. **The hook is your weapon.** "I'll build your site before we even meet" separates you from every other DM they get.
8. **Consistency beats intensity.** 10 connections a day for 30 days beats 100 in one day then nothing.
